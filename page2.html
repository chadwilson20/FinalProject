Binary Search

Number guessing game round 2

Let's explain binary search by using our number guessing game. Let's say that you picked the number that is right in the middle of the set of numbers (in our case 5). Let's also say that that is not the right number but after that you were given whether the number is greater than or less than five. What is the logical solution from this? To pick 8 if the number is greater than five or pick 2 if it is less than. You keep doing this until you have found the right number. This is the idea of binary search, to keep halving the numbers until you have found the right one.
